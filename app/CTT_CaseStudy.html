CTT ticket sales

Online ticket sales, redesigned.

Redesigned a ticket sales platform that makes the buying process for any kind of event a very straightforward task.

CTT has a history of nearly 500 years and it is one of the most iconic brands in Portugal. With a significant volume of sales, CTT Online Ticket Sales is a major driver of the company's growth.

  • Challenge

    Make the platform easier to navigate, turn the shopping experience easier and more engaging, and consequently, improve conversion rates and better express the brand.

  • Scope

    • Content strategy
    • UI/UX Design
    • Information architecture

  • Solution

    Facilitate product finding and streamline the buying process through a slick and enhanced user interface design.
