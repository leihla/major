To be major doesn’t necessarily mean to be big.

It can be quite the opposite sometimes. Like all the tiny details that go into crafting a perfect experience.

Major is a Lisbon based digital design studio founded in 2016. Our purpose is to make it effortless for people to have great, meaningful experiences that empower their real lives. For us, it's all about results, and we'll find a way to get to them.

We believe in making a difference through powerful, constructive, worthwhile design experiences.
