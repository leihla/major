We’re telling our story through what we create.

And we’ll keep on doing it until we see the world how it’s supposed to be.

Major is a Lisbon based digital design studio founded in 2016.

Our purpose is to make it effortless for people to have great, meaningful experiences that empower their real lives. We do everything from extensive research and strategy to UX and UI.

At Major, making a forward-looking, social impact on the world around us is serious business. We want to tell stories that matter — your story.

We believe in making a difference through powerful, constructive, worthwhile design experiences. Our vision is clear-cut: to create a positive impact in people’s lives. And we’ll do it without compromising our key values and the authenticity that follows us throughout every single project we put our hands on.
